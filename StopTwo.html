ABOUT THE RESTAURANT

Sibelicious is the restaurant of the Academy for Hotel and Facility management where first and second-year students get their hands-on experience by doing practical training.

You can enjoy a culinary journey provided by your fellow students at lunch from Monday through Friday (walk-in from 12:00 to 12:30) and for dinner Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday (walk-in from 18:00 to 18:30).

Reservations can and need to be made through the Fork or at the Visitor Centre. Sibelicious’ claim to fame is that it hosted dinner for the Commissioner of the King and the mayors of the 5 biggest cities in Brabant. Come be their next guest!
