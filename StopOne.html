ABOUT THE PLANES

The plane exhibition in Horizon North is a private collection of 2 Tourism BUas professionals who have been working in the aviation industry for over 20 years. Some of the models you see are rare and old, as lots of airline companies pop up and then quickly disappear. From the first aircraft ever created and business-class supersonic aircrafts to the new Boeing 787, the collection provides a holistic overview of the history of the aviation industry. Can you spot the festive Football edition model?

Even without prior experience, you can evaluate airplane models yourself! Engines become bigger and bigger as years pass by, mainly because smaller engines produce a lot of noise. Therefore, bigger engines indicate that planes are newer. You can judge the value of a plane model by their weight - the heavier they are, the more expensive.
