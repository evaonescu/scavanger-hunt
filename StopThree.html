ABOUT THE BLIND WALLS GALLERY

Blind Walls Gallery is a city mural museum. More than 100 meaningful artworks tell the stories of our city. The project is a homage to the old walls that used to surround the medieval Breda, providing protection and standing as a sign of prestige. Most of the old monuments have disappeared now and the blind walls that remained give the city a dull and grey appearance. Sadly, people often use these places to recklessly park their bicycles or even as a public toilet after a night out. Blind Walls Gallery wants to change that, one mural at a time. Have you spotted these artworks around town?  

Especially for the Leisure & Events course at Breda University of Applied Sciences, the art company Hedof created a design that aptly shows several leisure activities, like grooving, cruising or doing something soothing! The murals add colour and an overall high-toned vibe to the two stairwells on the right - and left sides of the building. The artist behind this funky piece is Rick Berkelmans. Check out his work at HEDOF!
